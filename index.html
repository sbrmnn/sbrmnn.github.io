Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I use RideBuddyNYC?
RideBuddyNYC connects travelers heading to or from NYC airports with others looking to share rides. Simply create or join a ride request and start chatting!

2. What airports are supported?
Currently, we support JFK, LaGuardia, and Newark airports.

3. How do I purchase a chat pass?
You can purchase a chat pass directly in the app under the "Chat Pass" section. New users get 30 days of free access!

4. How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?
Please email us at arun@yumfog.com with details about the bug or feature suggestion.
